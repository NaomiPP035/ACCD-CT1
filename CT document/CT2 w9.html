WEEK9 - Bulid for play

Lucky Claw is a very simple and easy-to-play claw game. The game's graphics are in the retro style of the 80s. The game mechanism mimics those annoying claw machines in the real world, as they are all very difficult to catch the dolls. The goal of Lucky Claw is to catch as many dolls as possible within the limited time. The game requires a physical arcade joystick and button, but it's fine if you don't have one, though you might miss out on some fun.
